Psychology Today

Torres Rey Eduard October 199, 2019 No Comments

This list wouldn’t be complete without Psychology Today. Also a magazine, the blog features contributions from many experts in the field. Posts range from topics like how psychology can be applied in daily life to overcoming psychological challenges. The blog’s website is very organized, offering several different topics to select from to help narrow your search. Beyond the blog, the website also offers resources such as full descriptions of mental disorders and a search engine to find a therapist in your area. On the downside, the website seems to be lacking in the design department, as most of the graphics look like they have not changed since 2005 — and all of the pictures seem to be stock photos. But for what it lacks in looks, it makes up for in the quality of posts, displaying several articles each day and providing interesting information right at your fingertips.

Anxious No More

Torres Rey Eduard October 18, 2019 No Comments

Focusing on the topic of anxiety, this blog offers an in-depth analysis on anxiety while offering ways to cope with it. Aimée is a blogger who personally deals with anxiety. She uses the blog as a therapeutic way of working through her anxiety by sharing her knowledge with others. One thing Aimée suggests people with anxiety do is create a “Personal Affirmation Flipbook” to remind them of why they are special. Even though the blog hasn’t been updated since 2012, it still has relevant information that reveals a lot about anxiety.
